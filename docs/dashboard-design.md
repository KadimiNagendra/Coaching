# Dashboard Design

The Teacher Dashboard is the daily landing page.

## Metrics

- Total Students
- Active Students
- Today's Classes
- Monthly Income
- Monthly Expenses
- Net Profit
- Pending Fees
- Attendance Present Today
- Attendance Absent Today
- Upcoming Exams
- Recent Payments

## Charts

- Monthly Income Trend
- Monthly Expense Trend
- Profit Trend
- Attendance Analytics
- Fee Collection Analytics

The frontend currently includes income, expense, and profit trend visualization with Recharts and dashboard cards connected to live backend summary data.
